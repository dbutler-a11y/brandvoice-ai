## 8. HOW IT WORKS ‚Äî UNIFIED CLIENT JOURNEY

This is the complete step-by-step flow from prospect to delivered content. This section unifies the client experience with internal campaign phases.

### STEP 1: DISCOVERY & BOOKING
**What the client sees:** "Book a Call" or "Get Started"
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Lead captured in CRM
- [ ] Discovery call scheduled (Calendly)
- [ ] Call conducted - confirm fit, package, and add-ons
- [ ] Payment collected via Stripe

**Deliverable:** Signed client, payment received

---

### STEP 2: ONBOARDING & VOICE SELECTION
**What the client sees:** Intake form + Voice Preview tool
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Client completes intake form:
  - Business name, niche, offers
  - Target audience
  - Brand tone (playful, luxury, serious, educational)
  - Preferred platforms (IG, TikTok, YouTube, FB)
  - Avatar preferences (gender, age range, style)
- [ ] Client uses on-site Voice Preview (ElevenLabs) to select voice tone
- [ ] Admin creates client record in dashboard
- [ ] Client linked to User account for portal access

**Deliverable:** Complete client profile, voice selection confirmed

---

### STEP 3: AVATAR & VOICE CREATION
**What the client sees:** Status update - "Creating Your AI Spokesperson"
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Generate avatar using VidBuzz / HeyGen / KIE / Kling
- [ ] Configure voice in ElevenLabs (preset or light clone)
- [ ] Save avatar assets to client folder
- [ ] Internal review for quality and brand fit

**Deliverable:** Approved avatar + voice ready for production

---

### STEP 4: SCRIPTWRITING
**What the client sees:** Status update - "Writing Your Scripts"
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Generate 30 scripts using AI + manual tweaks
- [ ] Mix content types:
  - Educational content
  - FAQs
  - Objection handling
  - CTAs
- [ ] Optional: Send for client approval (simple Yes/No or "Revise #X")
- [ ] Finalize scripts

**Deliverable:** 30 approved scripts ready for video production

---

### STEP 5: VIDEO PRODUCTION
**What the client sees:** Status update - "Producing Your Videos"
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Generate videos using HeyGen / VidBuzz / KIE / Kling
- [ ] Combine scripts + voice + avatar
- [ ] Ensure branding consistency
- [ ] Add viral-style captions (Captions.ai / Veed.io / AutoCaption)
- [ ] Export in required formats (9:16 primary)

**Deliverable:** 30 captioned videos ready for QA

---

### STEP 6: QUALITY ASSURANCE & REVIEW
**What the client sees:** Status update - "Final Review"
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Internal QA check on all videos
- [ ] Verify captions, audio sync, branding
- [ ] Organize files by day (Day 1‚Äì30)
- [ ] Prepare download package

**Deliverable:** QA-approved content package

---

### STEP 7: DELIVERY
**What the client sees:** "Your Content is Ready!" + Portal access
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Upload videos to Supabase Storage (client folder)
- [ ] Update ClientAssets in database
- [ ] Mark project status as "Delivered"
- [ ] Send delivery notification email
- [ ] Client can now log into portal to download all files

**Deliverable:** Client has full access to all content via portal

---

### STEP 8: ONGOING (Monthly Clients Only)
**What the client sees:** Monthly strategy call + new content in portal
**What happens internally:**
- [ ] Monthly strategy call
- [ ] Repeat Steps 4-7 for new content
- [ ] Update portal with new videos
- [ ] Track subscription renewal

**Deliverable:** Fresh 30 videos each month
